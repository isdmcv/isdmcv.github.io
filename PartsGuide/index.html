Genuine Parts

MMPC New Parts Online Inquiry

The new and improved Parts Inquiry designed for MMPC Dealers to get the realtime order status, parts inqury, unreleased shippings, back-order, credit line and more.

User Interface

Transitioning from green-screen interface to the new user interface may overwhelm the user. But with the interface patterned to be straight to the point, simple and light, and content first principle, it's never been easier, understandable and user friendlier than before.

Parts Inquiry

Just by entering the Part Number and clicking the view button, the software will get all needed data from MMPC Server and show it in an elegent way.

If the Parts happen to be in the back order, the software will show your priority number and its reliable Estimated Time of Arrival.

Pictures will also help identify the parts easier.

Multi-Parts Inquiry

Inquiry multiple Parts at once!

Reliable Estimated Time of Parts Arrival will also be displayed based on the next Unallocated Available Shipments.

*Data can be download to excel file.

Order Status

No need to call MMPC to get the status of your orders!

Unserve Parts will have their Estimated Time of Arrival to be be computed!

*Data can be download to excel file.

Under Picking

All of your under picking parts will be displayed with just a one click of a button.

*Data can be download to excel file.

Shipping Data

Download/Display Invoice realtime Shipping Data

*Downloadable file can be use to dealer existing system to automate their receiving process.

Download Shipping Data via Invoice Number Range

Download/Display Shipping Data using Invoice Number Range, example: 550290 to 550350

Download Shipping Data via Date Range

Download Display via Invoice Date Range, view it by day, by week, by month or even by year.

Unreleased Shippings

All unreleased shipping data can be monitored.

Credit Line

Credit Line can be check with just one click of a button.

A software wide warning is going to notify you if your available credit line is less than 10%.

Back Order

Back Order can now be monitored and download with just one click of a button!
